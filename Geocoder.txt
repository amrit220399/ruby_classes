Geocoder GEM

1. Needed For GeoCoding and Reverse GeoCoding

GeoCoding : Convert Address Line to Coordinates which is Latitude/Longitude form

Reverse Geocoding : Convert Lat/Lng to Address Line containing City, State, Country and Pincode

2. Used To Store Location in the form of GeoPoint in the database

3. Work with Maps like GoogleMaps, MapMyIndia to mark the location markers if we 
	are working with Places or Navigation.
